## Source Attribution

The underlying factual holiday information was collected from publicly available sources, including:

### Central Bank of Sri Lanka

The Central Bank of Sri Lanka holiday calendars were used as a source for recent:

* Holiday dates
* Holiday names
* Public holiday classifications
* Bank holiday classifications
* Mercantile holiday classifications

Source website:

`https://www.cbsl.gov.lk/en/about/about-the-bank/bank-holidays`

### Department of Government Printing, Sri Lanka

Official calendar documents, Gazette publications, and historical holiday information were obtained through the Department of Government Printing website.

Source website:

`https://documents.gov.lk/`

### Government of Sri Lanka

Some original holiday declarations are issued by the relevant ministries and public authorities of the Government of Sri Lanka.

The original institutions remain the authoritative providers of this information.

## Transformations Performed

This project independently performs transformations including:

* Webpage and PDF extraction
* Optical character recognition where required
* Manual validation of difficult historical records
* Date-format standardization
* Holiday-name normalization
* Removal of duplicate records
* Consolidation of equivalent holiday names
* Combination of multiple holidays occurring on the same date
* Creation of public, bank, mercantile, Poya Day, weekend, and general holiday indicators
* Creation of a complete daily calendar beginning on January 1, 2005
* Automated validation and annual dataset updates
